    Established in 1996, the Muttha Group has significantly contributed to the architecture of Pune. From the very first foundation stone that was laid the group has incorporated sound and innovative design concepts, involved some of the world's best architects and designers, used state-of-the-art infrastructure to make sure that the projects are nothing short of perfection.

    Muttha group has developed many large townships in Pune area that include:
    Sakal Nagar in Baner
    Anand Nagar in Kothrud
    Atma Nagar in PCMC

    All of these have gone on to become important landmarks of the city and have been the recipients of many awards.

    The group implements many high-end projects in the IT, Commercial, Business, Residential, Premium and De Luxe property segments.

    Muttha group focuses on property development of the mixed built which integrates Residential, Retail and IT spaces into one either as a single building project or a cluster of buildings.

    The group’s vision is to develop properties over 7.5 million square feet within the mixed property segment by next couple of years.

    Design and Features

    This magnificent commercial structure has a character of its own. It stands tall and strong, with a futuristic design that is progressive and liberal. The entire outer façade lends the building a stunning character.

    • The Double Façade substantially reduces the energy consumption of the building.
    • Under EDGE Certification process from US GBC.
    • The building promotes an integrated environment which is conducive for businesses to collaborate.
    • The architecture is sustainable and eco-friendly.
    • A provision for handicapped washrooms on each floor.
    • A state of the art reception – complete with a visitor management system.
    • A cafeteria within the premise – Bespoke.
    • Orientation of the floor plate ensures ample day light in the offices thereby creating a lively environment on the floor.
    • Dedicated signage space available outside the building.
    Technical specifications
    • 100% Laminated Glass windows.
    • Extensive surveillance system on all common areas.
    • High speed dedicated internet connectivity.
    • Advanced Fire Protection system.
    • Reprographics on each floor.
    • Dedicated hospitality & facility management team.
    • High end and ergonomically proven furniture.
    • European Tile finishes in Common Areas and Toilets.
    • High Tech communication systems for conferencing.
    key distances
